## What is OpenTogetherTube?

OpenTogetherTube is a real-time video synchronization platform that lets you watch videos together with friends, no matter where they are. Create a room, add videos from multiple platforms, and enjoy perfectly synchronized playback with built-in chat.

**Try it now at [opentogethertube.com](https://opentogethertube.com/)**

<Info>
No account registration required. Create a room and start watching in seconds.
</Info>

## Key features

### Video playback

- **Real-time video synchronization** - Automatic sync keeps everyone watching at the same moment
- **SponsorBlock integration** - Automatically skip sponsored segments in YouTube videos
- **Vote to skip** - Democratic control over what plays next
- **DJ mode** - Perfect for background music during D&D sessions or hangouts

### Room management

- **Custom room URLs** - Create memorable, shareable links like `opentogethertube.com/my-movie-night`
- **Flexible permissions** - Control who can add videos, manage the queue, or change settings
- **Vote mode** - Let everyone vote on what to watch next
- **Queue management** - Add, reorder, and remove videos from the playlist

- **Multiple UI themes** - Choose from different visual themes to match your preference
