Get started watching videos together in just a few simple steps. No account required!

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit OpenTogetherTube">
    Go to [opentogethertube.com](https://opentogethertube.com/) in your web browser.
    
    You'll see the home page with options to create or join a room.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a room">
    Choose between two types of rooms:
    
    **Temporary Room**
    - Click **Create Temporary Room** for a quick session
    - Room name is automatically generated (e.g., `cheerful-elephant`)
    - Room expires after 2 hours of inactivity
    - No account needed
    
    **Permanent Room**
    - Click **Create Permanent Room** to customize the name
    - Enter a custom room name (e.g., `movie-night`)
    - Room persists indefinitely
    - Can be claimed with an account for ownership
    
    <Info>
      Room URLs are shareable: `https://opentogethertube.com/room/your-room-name`
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add videos">
    Once in your room, add videos to watch:
    
    1. Click the **Add Video** button or search box
    2. Enter a video URL or search query:
       - YouTube videos or playlists
       - Vimeo videos
       - Direct video URLs (.mp4, .webm)
       - HLS streams (.m3u8)
       - DASH streams (.mpd)
    3. Click **Add** to add the video to the queue
    
    <Tip>
      You can search YouTube directly by typing keywords instead of a URL
    </Tip>
    
    **Supported formats:**
    ```
    https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=...
    https://youtu.be/...
    https://vimeo.com/...
    https://example.com/video.mp4
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite friends">
    Share your room with friends:
    
    - **Copy the room URL** from your browser's address bar
    - **Click the Share button** in the room to get a direct link
    - Send the link via:
      - Discord, Slack, or other chat apps
      - Email or text message
      - Social media
    
    Everyone who joins will see the same video at the same time!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key features

### Playback controls

- **Play/Pause** - Everyone sees synchronized playback
- **Seek** - Jump to any point in the video
- **Skip** - Move to the next video in queue
- **Playback speed** - Adjust speed from 0.25x to 2x

### Room settings

Access room settings via the ⚙️ icon:

- **Title and description** - Customize your room
- **Visibility** - Public, unlisted, or private
- **Queue mode** - Manual, vote, loop, or DJ mode
- **Permissions** - Control who can add videos, chat, and more

<Note>
  Only the room owner or administrators can change room settings
</Note>

### Vote mode

Enable democratic video selection:

- Room members vote on videos in the queue
- Highest voted video plays next
- Great for group movie nights

### DJ mode

Enable continuous playback with auto-skip:

- Videos play automatically in order
- Perfect for background music while gaming or working
- No manual intervention needed

### SponsorBlock integration

Automatically skip sponsored segments:

- Works with YouTube videos
- Skip sponsor segments, intros, outros, and more
- Configurable in client settings

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Video won't play">
    - Check that the video URL is supported
    - Try refreshing the page
    - Check your browser's console for errors
    - Some videos may be region-blocked or require authentication
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Playback is out of sync">
    - Click your profile icon and select **Refresh**
    - Check your internet connection
    - Try reducing playback quality
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't add videos to queue">
    - Check room permissions - you may need to request **add-to-queue** permission
    - Contact the room owner or an administrator
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Room not found">
    - Verify the room URL is correct
    - Temporary rooms expire after 2 hours of inactivity
    - The room may have been deleted by its owner
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
